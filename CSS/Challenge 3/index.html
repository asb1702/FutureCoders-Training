What we do

Recruitment

After a rigorous selection process a small number are selected to join a development team, formed specifically to complete a development project led by our technical lead developer. Young developers learn to operate as effective members of our teams.

Development

We follow industry best practices, agile development processes, code reviews and test-driven development. Our young developers learn fast. All code has to pass through our quality monitoring process to prove they are bug-free and comply with requirements.

Delivery

We deliver high quality products with contributions from young developers fully reviewed. Not only do we produce great software solutions but also, great young developers, ready to be recommended for an apprenticeship and so reduce the skills gap, a little at a time.
